About the House

Born backstage.
Built for the floor.

Petrosyan Designs is a New York atelier devoted to one thing: professional dance costumes for ballroom competition and stage shows. Founded by designers Erik Petro and Maga Taryan, the house dresses champions, rising Pro-Am partnerships, and performers who refuse to blend into the ballroom.

Every gown and Latin dress is designed around the dancer — their silhouette, their movement, their music. Because nothing we make is sold off a rack, nothing we make exists twice.

The Consultation

From first fitting
to final sparkle

Every commission follows the same couture ritual — unhurried, collaborative, and entirely about you.

Step 01

Measurements Taken

We begin with a complete set of professional measurements — in person at the New York atelier or guided remotely — so the costume moves as a second skin.

Step 02

Styles Discussed

Your discipline, your music, your competition calendar, your personality on the floor. We talk silhouettes, color under ballroom light, and what the judges should remember.

Step 03

Examples Shown

We walk through past commissions, sketches, fabrics, and stonework so you can see — and touch — the direction before a single cut is made.

Step 04

Custom Design Created

Erik and Maga design and build your one-of-one piece by hand: pattern, construction, and crystal by crystal.

Step 05

Tweaked & Finalized

Fittings and refinements based on your feedback — on the body, in motion — until the costume is competition-ready and unmistakably yours.
